# Meta Goal: Experiment with AI-Assisted Design

The project is also a test bed for using AI (Claude) as a design collaborator.

## Facts

- Claude is used throughout: ingesting datasheets, structuring the knowledge base, generating design options, writing specs.
- The goal is to understand what AI-assisted design actually feels like in practice — where it helps, where it gets in the way, what the right division of labor is.
- This is not about automating decisions. It is about augmenting the thinking process: faster research, better-structured notes, a thinking partner that has read all the datasheets.
- Learning happens in both directions: hardware design skills for the human, prompt/workflow patterns for future projects.
