# Meta Goal: Inspire and Communicate

One purpose of the project is to be visible and shareable — not just a private build.

## Facts

- The finished object should be interesting enough to show to people and spark curiosity about DIY hardware, vintage things, and what you can do with a Raspberry Pi and a soldering iron.
- Documentation and process matter as much as the result. A well-told build story is part of the deliverable.
- Audience: curious generalists, makers, people who liked radio as a kid but don't usually do hardware.
- "Communicating about" means: write-up, photos, maybe a short video. Not a product launch.
