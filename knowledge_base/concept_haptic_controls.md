# Concept: Haptic Control Quality

Sourcing physical controls that match the "heavy, intentional, mechanical" feel of vintage hardware.

## Facts
- Standard EC11 rotary encoders feel plasticky — avoid for main controls.
- **Elma Electronic:** "Swissclick" rotary switches/encoders; used in professional studio gear; millions-of-cycles rating.
- **Grayhill 51 Series:** crisp, heavy detent; favorite in maker community for "overbuilt" feel.
- **NKK Switches:** rugged toggles/pushbuttons; cockpit-grade snap.
- **Bourns Pro Audio:** smooth, damped travel for volume/slider use.
- **Programmable haptics (future):** XeelTech HAPTICORE (electromagnetic virtual detents), Titan Haptics (LMR motors). Overkill for v1 but possible.
- Mechanical keyboard switches (Gateron Oil King, Gazzew U4T) viable for individual function buttons.
- LoveMySwitches: boutique distributor for pedal/synth builders — curated for heaviness.
