# Component: Amplifier

Drives the speaker from the DAC output.

## Facts
- Original speaker is 1W / 25Ω — retention TBD.
- Esparagus board (ESP32 option) includes a Class-D amp onboard.
- For Pi option: separate Class-D amp module needed.
- Separate power rail from the Pi/ESP32 recommended to prevent ground loop noise.
- Cabinet interior should be lined with foam to reduce acoustic boxiness.
