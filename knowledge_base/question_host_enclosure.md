# Question: Host Enclosure — Vintage Radio or Custom Case?

What physical shell does the device live in?

## Facts
- Option A: retrofit a vintage transistor radio (e.g. Mondivox 705). Gives retro feel for free; constrained by existing geometry.
- Option B: custom-built case (3D print or fabrication). Full control over geometry; retro feel must be designed in deliberately.
- The vintage shell is a strong preference but not a hard requirement.
- Other vintage radio brands considered: Sonolor (Ranger, Vogue, Vero54), Reela, Telefunken, Optalix (TO99/TO100/TO305), Radiola, Philips, Schneider.
